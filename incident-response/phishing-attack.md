# Incident Response Report – Phishing Attack Simulation
Author: Arslan Masud

## 1. Overview
A phishing simulation was conducted to evaluate employee awareness and incident response readiness.

## 2. Incident Summary
- A fake “password reset” email was sent to 20 employees.
- 6 employees clicked the link.
- 2 employees submitted credentials.

## 3. Response Actions
- Identified affected accounts
- Forced password resets
- Reviewed login logs
- Conducted awareness training

## 4. Lessons Learned
- Need stronger MFA enforcement
- Need regular phishing awareness training
- Improve email filtering rules
